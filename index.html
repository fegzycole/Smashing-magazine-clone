PERSON OF THE WEEK

Ire Aderinokun is a self-taught Front-End Developer and User Interface Designer from Lagos, Nigeria. She is currently the co-founder, COO, and VP Engineering of BuyCoins, a cryptocurrency exchange for Africa, and previously worked with eyeo, the company behind products like Adblock Plus and Flattr Plus. Ire is a Google Expert, specializing in the core front-end technologies HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, but is passionate about all aspects of technology.
