PERSON OF THE WEEK

Ire Aderinokun is a self-taught Front-End Developer and User Interface Designer from Lagos, Nigeria. She is currently the co-founder, COO, and VP Engineering of BuyCoins, a cryptocurrency exchange for Africa, and previously worked with eyeo, the company behind products like Adblock Plus and Flattr Plus. Ire is a Google Expert, specializing in the core front-end technologies HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, but is passionate about all aspects of technology. She writes about these topics on her blog and regularly shares her knowledge speaking at conferences around Africa and the world. Thank you for everything you do for the community, dear Ire!
